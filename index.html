materials used in agriculture

    



    
1.Seeds:

    
High-quality seeds are the foundation of agriculture. They are used to grow crops, vegetables, fruits, and flowers. 
        Different varieties of seeds are available depending on the desired plant characteristics.


2.Fertilizers: 


Fertilizers provide essential nutrients to plants, promoting growth and increasing yield. 
    They can be organic (such as compost, manure) or synthetic (including nitrogen, phosphorus, and potassium compounds).


3.Pesticides:


Pesticides are used to control pests, insects, weeds, and diseases that can damage crops. 
    They come in various forms, including insecticides, herbicides, fungicides, and rodenticides.


4.Herbicides:


Herbicides specifically target and control weeds that compete with crops for nutrients, water, and sunlight. 
    They help maintain crop health and yield.


5.Irrigation systems:


Water is crucial for plant growth, and irrigation systems help provide controlled water supply to agricultural fields. 
    Common irrigation methods include sprinkler systems, drip irrigation, and flood irrigation.
